Last weekend, I went to the park with my best friend, Tom. The weather was warm and sunny, so there were many people in the park. Some children were playing on the playground, and some old people were taking a walk. We walked along the lake and enjoyed the beautiful scenery. We talked about our school life and our dreams. At noon, we had lunch in a small restaurant near the park. The food was delicious and not expensive. In the afternoon, we went to the library to read books. I read a book about animals, and Tom read a science book. We stayed there for two hours. When I got home, I felt a little tired but very happy. It was a wonderful weekend that I will never forget.
