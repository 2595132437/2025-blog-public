Starting from last month, I have kept doing morning exercise every day. Every morning, I get up at 6:30 and go to the neighborhood square. There are many people doing exercise there, such as running, doing tai chi and jumping rope. I usually run for 20 minutes first, then do some stretching exercises. The morning air is very fresh, and the sun is gentle. When I run, I can enjoy the beautiful scenery around me and listen to light music. Morning exercise not only makes my body strong, but also makes me energetic all day. It helps me focus on my study better. I think everyone should keep doing morning exercise. It is a good habit that can bring us health and happiness.
