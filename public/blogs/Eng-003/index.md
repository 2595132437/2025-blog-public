My hometown is a small and beautiful town. It is located in the south of China. There are many green trees and clear rivers in my hometown. The air is fresh, and the people are kind and friendly. Most people in my hometown are farmers. They work hard in the fields every day and grow rice, vegetables and fruits. There is a small square in the center of the town. Every evening, many people go there to dance or chat. There is also a primary school and a hospital in our town, which makes our life very convenient. I love my hometown very much. It is the most beautiful place in my heart, and I hope it will become more and more beautiful in the future.
