# 两个月英语背诵计划（覆盖初中、高中、四级词汇）

计划说明：本计划共60天，分3个阶段推进，循序渐进覆盖初中1600核心词、高中3500核心词（含初中词汇）、四级4500核心词（含初高中词汇）。每天1篇背诵文章，字数根据阶段调整（从200词逐步提升至450词），生词率控制在5%-10%，每篇文章标注核心词汇（区分初/高/四级），兼顾口语练习与词汇记忆，每天背诵后可结合之前的“五步精背法”消化，确保词汇真正掌握、会用。

## 第一阶段（第1-20天：夯实初中词汇，衔接高中基础）

核心目标：巩固初中1600核心词，初步接触高中基础词汇（约500个），文章以简单记叙文、日常对话为主，字数200-250词，生词以初中高频词为主，穿插少量高中基础词。

### 第1天

背诵文章：My Daily Life

I get up at 7 o'clock every morning. After brushing my teeth and washing my face, I have a simple breakfast with my family. My mother often prepares bread and milk for me, which is quick and nutritious. Then I go to school by bike. It takes me about 15 minutes to get there. At school, I have six classes a day. My favorite subject is English because my English teacher is kind and patient. After school, I usually do my homework first. When I finish it, I play basketball with my friends for half an hour. It makes me relaxed. In the evening, I read an English storybook before going to bed. I think my daily life is busy but happy.

核心词汇（初中）：daily, brush, breakfast, nutritious, bike, subject, patient, homework, relax, evening, storybook

### 第2天

背诵文章：A Happy Weekend

Last weekend, I went to the park with my best friend, Tom. The weather was warm and sunny, so there were many people in the park. At noon, we had lunch in a small restaurant near the park. The food was delicious and not expensive. In the afternoon, we went to the library to read books. I read a book about animals, and Tom read a science book. We stayed there for two hours. When I got home, I felt a little tired but very happy. It was a wonderful weekend that I will never forget.
